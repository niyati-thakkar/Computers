Computers - THEN & NOW
Although computers seem like a relatively new invention, the history of data processing dates back to the early 19th century.
As personal computers became popular in the 1970s, the Xerox Alto helped pave the way for Steve Jobs to his Apple.
Laptops became more popular in the early 2000s, especially after his Apple released his MacBook Air.
Computers today come in all shapes and sizes, including tablets.
